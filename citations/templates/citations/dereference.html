An identifier can also be de-reference through the OCCUR API:

www.occur.duckdns.org/citations/dereference/?identifier=$IDENTIFIER

Explanation
Paste an OCCUR identifier (either PK or UID) to de-reference an identifier. OCCUR will verify that the data has remained unchanged since the creation of the identity. If the data has changed, the user will be provided with a warning that the identifier cannot be de-referenced anymore. Otherwise, OCCUR will redirect to the data.
