A citation can also be de-reference through the OCCUR API:

www.occur.duckdns.org/citations/dereference/?identifier=$IDENTIFIER

Explanation
Paste an OCCUR identifier (either PK or UID) to de-reference a citation. OCCUR will verify that the data has remained unchanged since the creation of the citation. If the data has changed, the user will be provided with a warning that the citation cannot be de-referenced anymore. Otherwise, OCCUR will redirect to the data.
