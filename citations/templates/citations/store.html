Store a Citation

A citation can also be saved through the OCCUR API:

www.occur.duckdns.org/citations/store/?dap_url=$DAP_URL

Explanation

Paste a DAP URL to retrieve a permanent citation.

If a citation for the same data has already been stored, OCCUR will redirect to this previously stored citation. Otherwise, a new citation reference will be stored.

A citation is defined solely by the query to the data (url), the timestamp of the citation creation, and the hash of the data at the time of citation creation.
